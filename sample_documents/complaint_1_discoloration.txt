Apollo Pharmacy reported discolored capsules in Amoxicillin Capsules 500mg.
Batch number AMX240602. Manufacturing date March 2026. Expiry date February 2028.
A sealed bottle was found to contain 12 capsules with visible yellow-brown discoloration
out of a total of 28 capsules affected across the reported stock. Apollo Pharmacy is
requesting an investigation and replacement of the affected stock.
